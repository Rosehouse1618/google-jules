Our Policies

Important information about living in ILM Student Halls

Booking and Payment Policies

All bookings must be made at least 2 weeks in advance of the intended move-in date. A deposit of Â£200 is required to secure your room, which will be refunded upon departure (subject to room inspection).

  • Rent is due on the 1st of each month
  • Late payments may result in additional charges
  • Payment methods accepted: Bank transfer, credit/debit card
  • Minimum stay period: 1 semester
Important: Failure to pay rent on time may result in eviction proceedings.

House Rules and Conduct

We maintain a respectful and safe environment for all residents. The following rules must be followed:

  • Quiet hours: 11:00 PM - 7:00 AM
  • No smoking in buildings (designated outdoor areas available)
  • No pets allowed (except registered assistance animals)
  • No parties or large gatherings without prior permission
  • Respect for other residents' privacy and property
  • Proper disposal of rubbish and recycling

Room Maintenance and Repairs

We are committed to maintaining high standards of accommodation. Maintenance requests should be reported immediately:

  • Emergency repairs: 24/7 hotline available
  • Non-emergency repairs: Within 48 hours
  • Regular cleaning service provided weekly
  • Residents responsible for keeping rooms tidy
  • Damage beyond normal wear and tear will be charged

Security and Safety

Your safety is our priority. We have implemented several security measures:

  • 24/7 CCTV monitoring in common areas
  • Secure entry system with key cards
  • Regular security patrols
  • Fire safety equipment and regular drills
  • Emergency contact numbers posted in all rooms
Emergency: In case of emergency, call 999 immediately, then notify hall management.

Visitors and Guests

We welcome visitors but ask for consideration of other residents:

  • Day visitors: No registration required
  • Overnight guests: Maximum 2 nights per week
  • Guests must be accompanied by residents at all times
  • Residents responsible for their guests' behavior
  • Large gatherings require 48-hour notice

Cancellation and Refund Policy

We understand that circumstances may change. Our cancellation policy is as follows:

  • More than 30 days notice: Full refund of deposit
  • 15-30 days notice: 50% refund of deposit
  • Less than 15 days notice: No refund
  • Early departure: Pro-rated refund for unused rent
  • Force majeure events: Full refund
